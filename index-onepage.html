Mobile Optimization

The mobile optimization feature will ensure the website is fully accessible on smartphones and tablets.   • Information Service Portal
  • Online Service Request Forms

By digitizing these services, the website will help reduce paperwork, improve service delivery, and enhance public satisfaction.

"Development of an Inventory Management System for Disaster Relief Distribution at the Garut Regional Disaster Management Agency"

This project aims to design and develop a comprehensive inventory management system specifically for the Garut Regional Disaster Management Agency (BPBD). The system will streamline the process of managing and monitoring stock for disaster relief, ensuring that essential supplies are efficiently distributed to the affected areas. The application will also provide real-time data on the distribution process, allowing for tracking and visualization of how much aid has been delivered and ensuring that resources are allocated effectively.

Mobile Optimization

The mobile optimization feature will ensure the website is fully accessible on smartphones and tablets. With a responsive design, touch-friendly navigation, and fast loading speeds, users can easily interact with the site on any device. Service request forms will be mobile-friendly, and mobile-first features will ensure key actions like form submissions and status tracking are easily accessible. Push notifications will keep users updated on their requests, offering a seamless and convenient experience.

Key Features

  • Inventory Management
  • Real-time Stock Monitoring
  • Disaster Relief Distribution Tracking
  • Mapping and Geographic Tracking
  • Reporting and Analytics
  • Role-based Access Control

With this system, BPBD Garut will be able to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of its disaster relief efforts. The real-time tracking, inventory management, and geographic mapping will provide transparency, accountability, and faster response times, ensuring that aid reaches the affected areas quickly and efficiently. The application will also help BPBD to make informed decisions, prioritize resources, and maintain effective coordination during disaster relief operations.
